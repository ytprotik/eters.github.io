INTRODUCING OURSELVES

The first ever society of the Department of Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering,
established in 2022 with a vision to grow and nurture the habit and passion of research among the students and guide those who are involved in research.

So far quite a few seminars, webinars and workshops have been arranged and each of them has seen an exceptionally high rate of participation among students.

The society boasts a highly active executive panel consisting of an apt and dedicated team of ETE students and faculty members.
